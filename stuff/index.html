About us.

.

.

Kant Goyal & Associates ('The Firm') is a partnership firm constituted in February 1980, comprising of practicing Chartered Accountants providing services to individual Clients, Banks and Corporates . The Firm operates through its Head Office at Ambala and Branches in Chandigarh and Patiala with a motto to maintain ethical, professional and technical Standards by adopting quality control procedures.
The Firm comprises of Three partners - CA. S.P. Goyal, CA. Mohit Goyal and CA. Shaifali Goyal. Our firm also consists of one Ex-Bankers.
The Firm has been set up to serve clients of stature and profile. The offices are staffed by a combination of local recruits and experienced personnel trained with them. In this way control is maintained over methodology and quality while capitalising on local business knowledge.
